Tribe was created to encourage open conversation, self-expression and community action at OutboundEngine. We have three sub-groups in Tribe: Women, People of Color, and LGBTQ+. Each group hosts quarterly gatherings in which interested employees can participate in discussion lead by employees and/or local speakers. This is a safe-space to ask questions and better connect with co-workers on pertinent issues.

Tribe Women promotes equality in the workplace for women by helping women be heard, empowering women to negotiate for salaries and promotions, and educating allies on how to quell their internal biases

Tribe LGBTQ+ promotes equality in the workplace for LGBTQ+ people by helping them be better understood by their peers, dispelling stereotypes and discomfort around LGBTQ+ topics, and educating allies on how to quell their internal biases

Tribe POC promotes equality in the workplace for people of color by encouraging diversity in hiring practices, empowering people of color in leadership roles, and educating allies people on how to quell their internal biases
